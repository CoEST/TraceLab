UC36	Number:	UC36
Name:	Make Call
Authors:	N.B., C.P., S.W., C.A.
Event:	Caller wishes to call callee.
Callee’s phone number as input.
Actors:	Caller
Overview:	This use case captures the process by which the caller places a call to the callee.  The caller picks up the phone, receives a dial tone, and then proceeds to dial 4 digits to make the call.  
If the caller’s account is cancelled, then they do not receive a dial tone.  If the caller’s account is suspended, then they receive a dial tone, but cannot call anyone but the administrator.
If the callee is busy, then the caller receives a busy signal.  If the callee is not busy, then the caller hears the ringing signal until the callee picks up the phone, or until five minutes pass and the system drops the connection.~
